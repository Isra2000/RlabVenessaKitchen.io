"We serve with love"

Vision

20 odd years in the business , Vanessa and her close knit team has dedicated their time and lives to serving the community of Rocklands and many more by setting up feeding schemes and has hosted events that has aided the community .

Mission

Vanessa and her team has committed themselves to aiding to the community's needs and has an on going feeding scheme every Wednesday , hoping to feed as many mouths as possible . Om average , they would feed 500+ people every Wednesday .

Challenges

In these turbulent times , many households across all communities has been rocked by the pandemic as well as continuous social issues .In reality , there are families who often miss meals due to financial issues and that is where Vanessa's kitchen tries their best to feed as many mouths as they can .

Vanessa's kitchen in action

The feeding scheme

For the past 20 years , Vanessa's Kicthen has never missed A Wednesday and as of recent , Vanessa's kitchen serves food on every other day of the week.

Donations

The organization is very blessed to receive donations from every direction both from other organizations and from members of the community

More than just a feeding scheme

Vanessa has taken it upon herself to avail herself for one on one counselling with members of the community who seeks a safe space to speak openly and freely
